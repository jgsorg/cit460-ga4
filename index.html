Pine Creek Ceramics

About Me

Hi, I’m Maegan McLaughlin, the potter behind Pine Creek Ceramics. I make handmade, small-batch pottery inspired by quiet mornings, evergreen forests, and the things that make a house feel like home.


I create mugs that fit your hands just right, bowls that become kitchen staples, and vases that brighten a shelf even when they’re empty.


Everything is made by me from start to finish. They are thrown, trimmed, glazed, and fired in small runs, so no two items are exactly the same.
